How it works

Real-time resources,
from real people

Mālama Map is community-powered. Churches, organizations, businesses, and individuals post and update their own listings — so you always know what's available, where it is, and whether it's still open.

01

Your community posts a listing

A church, org, or individual creates a free account, adds their address, what they're offering or accepting, and their hours. Takes 2 minutes.

02

It shows up on the map instantly

Their pin goes live right away. Listings auto-expire after 72 hours with a renewal reminder — so the map always stays accurate and current.

03

Open / closed in real time

Every location has a simple Open/Closed toggle. People know before they make the drive — no wasted trips, no confusion.

Verified organizations earn a verified badge by confirming their registration through the Hawaii Business Registry. Duplicate locations at the same address require admin approval — keeping the map clean and trustworthy.
